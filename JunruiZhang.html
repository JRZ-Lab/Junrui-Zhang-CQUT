Research Focus

  • Long-term research on medical applications of micro-nano processing-based sensors and their dedicated ICs has resulted in a series of representative achievements, including CMOS-compatible three-dimensional skin sweat analysis chips, organ surface sensors based on flexible conductive polymer composites, brain - machine interface electrodes, and electronic blood vessels.
  • The inventor has obtained multiple patents and transferred them to Swiss high - tech companies, creating a number of jobs and raising over 200 million RMB in total.
  • Collaborated with well - known businesses (such as Abbott) and research institutions (for example, the world - class microelectronics institute Fraunhofer IZM and the top - 10 Swiss University of Lausanne Hospital) on projects with a total budget of about 30 million euros.
